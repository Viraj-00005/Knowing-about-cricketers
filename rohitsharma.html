Description

Rohit Sharma is one of the most elegant and destructive batsmen in modern cricket. He is famous for his effortless batting style, timing, and ability to score big hundreds.


He is known as the "Hitman" of Indian cricket and holds several world records, including the highest individual score in ODIs (264 runs). He has played many match-winning innings for India across all formats.
