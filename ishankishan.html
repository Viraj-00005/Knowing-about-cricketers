Description

Ishan Kishan is an Indian wicketkeeper-batsman known for his aggressive batting style. He plays as a top-order batter and is capable of scoring quick runs in limited-overs cricket. He is one of the young emerging talents in Indian cricket.


He gained attention after scoring a double century in ODI cricket against Bangladesh. Kishan is known for his fearless approach and attacking shots from the start. He continues to develop as an important player for India in all formats.
