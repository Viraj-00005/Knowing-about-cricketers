Description

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest modern-day batsmen in cricket history. He is known for his consistency, aggression, and strong batting technique. He has performed exceptionally in all formats of international cricket.


Kohli has played many match-winning innings for India under pressure. He is also known for his fitness, discipline, and leadership skills. His dedication and passion have made him a global cricket icon.
