Description

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest batsmen in cricket history. He is known for his aggressive batting style, consistency, fitness, and leadership skills. Kohli represented India in all formats of cricket and became one of the most successful players in the world.


He has broken many international records and inspired millions of cricket fans globally through his performances and dedication. Virat Kohli is widely regarded as one of the best ODI batsmen of all time and has played many match-winning innings for India.
