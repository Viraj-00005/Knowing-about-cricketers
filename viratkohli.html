Virat Kohli is one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He is famous for his aggressive batting style, consistency, fitness, and leadership skills. Kohli has represented India in all formats of cricket and has broken many world records. He became one of the fastest players to score thousands of international runs and is admired by fans across the world. His dedication and passion for cricket made him one of the most successful captains and batsmen in modern cricket.
