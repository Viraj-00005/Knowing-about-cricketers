Description

Sachin Tendulkar is regarded as one of the greatest cricketers in the history of cricket. He is famous for his outstanding batting skills, consistency, and long international career. Millions of cricket fans around the world call him the “God of Cricket”.


Sachin played for India for more than two decades and broke many world records. He became the first player to score 100 international centuries and over 34000 international runs. His dedication, discipline, and humble personality made him a global sports icon.
