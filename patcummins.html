Description

Pat Cummins is one of the best fast bowlers in modern cricket and the captain of the Australian cricket team. He is known for his accurate pace bowling, calm leadership, and strong performances in international matches. Cummins has played a major role in many victories for Australia across all formats.


Apart from bowling, Pat Cummins is also a useful lower-order batsman who can score important runs. He helped Australia win major ICC tournaments and became one of the most respected captains in world cricket. His consistency, sportsmanship, and teamwork have made him a global cricket star.
